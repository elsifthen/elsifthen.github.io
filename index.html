Introspection

a 5 part long-form generative art series created by elsif

 

Day by day, memories accumulate and shape our dreams, subconscious, ideals, and ultimately, the innermost self.

I. Memory Module

Nostalgic, like an old sketch made with pencil and ink. Someone, someplace, a feeling or a melody, in a fleeting moment that I wish to hold on to.

II. Dream Module

Fantastical, glowing and fading, as if they were from another dimension. In our dreams we stay still in space, and the thin thread of time spreads out, surrounding us with the past present and future fused together.

Mysterious with unfathomable depths.
